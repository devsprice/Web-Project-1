Our instructors

Brooks

Brooks has been practicing Jiu-Jitsu for over 20 years, he was born and raised in Maine. He started competing as a blue belt and has taken gold in every local competition he has competed in. As for world competition he's a 4x IBJJF champion and a 2x ADCC champion, a 3x EBI winner, and a WNO winner. He no longer competes professionally and now dedicated his life to teaching Jiu-Jitsu and is the head instructor of the Gym.

Will

Will has been doing Jiu-Jitsu for over 12 years and got his black belt while training under Brooks, Will busted on the scene as a purple belt and won worlds as the oldest competitor in his brackett. He won 2 gold and 1 silver medals as a brown belt all by submission, he is known for his grit and toughness. He is the head instructor of kids class.

Jason

Jason has been training Jiu-Jitsu for about 10 years and is currently a brown belt, he is the assistant instructor to the kids class. Jason entered the open weight brackett as a blue belt and took gold. He won worlds as a blue and purple belt, and is in training to win worlds as a brown belt. He has been in 5 MMA fights and won 4 by submission and 1 by TKO.
